Heroes

This is a collection of my favorite heroes.

Wei

The State of Wei is the most powerful of the Three Kingdoms. Built on Cao Cao’s legacy, it dominated northern China and played a central role in the era’s power struggles before falling to the Jin Dynasty in 265 CE.

Cao Cao

"An unscrupulous northern warlord"

Tips: Great choice if your role is Monarch.

Sima Yi

"A shrewd general and strategist who rose to power in Wei and laid the groundwork for the Jin Dynasty"

Tips: Wait for Scroll Lightning before using your ability

Shu

The Kingdom of Shu is founded by Liu Bei and guided by Zhuge Liang.

Liu Bei

"Benevolent Shu Lord"

Tips: Give cards to others before the discard phase

Zhuge Liang

"Loyal and intelligent minister"

Tips: Use Astrology to change judgment draws

Zhao Yun

"Full of courage"

Tips: Prioritize keeping a dodge card in your hand

Wu

The kingdom in the southeast of China, a land of prosperity and talent.

Sun Quan

"Crave peace but is not afraid to fight."

Tips: Use skill Balance every turn

Huang Gai

"The model of sacrifice"

Tips: lose points to lanuch an unstoppable counterattack
