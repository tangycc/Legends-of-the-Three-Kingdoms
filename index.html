Legends of the Three Kingdoms

Welcome to the ultimate website for playing LTR

Romance of the Three Kingdoms is a classic Chinese historical novel written by Luo Guanzhong in the 14th century. Set in the turbulent years at the end of the Han Dynasty, it dramatizes the power struggles between the kingdoms of Wei, Shu, and Wu. It's a book about history and legends.

The Legends of the Three Kingdoms is a strategic card game inspired by the classic Chinese novel Romance of the Three Kingdoms. Players take on the roles of famous historical figures and use unique abilities to outwit their opponents. The game captures heroism of the Three Kingdoms era through immersive gameplay.

This website is your comprehensive guide to learn how to play this card game!
